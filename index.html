About Us

Welcome to Loch Quarry Outdoor Centre, your gateway to adventure in the heart of Scotland!

For over 45 years, since 1978, we've been inspiring individuals, students, young people, school groups, and families to discover the thrill of the great outdoors. Our legacy is built on creating safe, engaging, and unforgettable experiences for every adventurer. Whether you're seeking excitement during our vibrant summer sessions or invigorating winter challenges, Loch Quarry offers something unique for everyone. Dive into our diverse range of activities:

  • Land Adventures: Conquer mountain biking trails, explore scenic hiking routes, and scale rock climbing ascents.
  • Water Adventures: Paddle across stunning lochs with kayaking and canoeing, or try the balance of stand-up paddleboarding.
  • Rope Adventures: Challenge your limits on high ropes courses, thrilling zip lines, and exhilarating abseiling experiences.

Our passionate and certified instructors are dedicated to ensuring your safety and maximizing your fun, fostering confidence and a love for nature.
