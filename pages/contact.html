Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit?

Loch Quarry is open year-round! Summer (June-August) offers warm weather perfect for water activities. Spring and autumn provide beautiful scenery, while winter brings unique experiences like ice climbing and winter hiking.

Do I need experience for the activities?

No! We offer activities for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced adventurers. Our instructors are trained to work with everyone and will tailor the experience to your abilities.

What should I bring?

We provide most equipment needed for activities. We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, waterproof jacket, sturdy shoes, and a water bottle. Specific activity details will be provided upon booking.

Are there age restrictions?

Most activities are suitable for ages 8+. Some advanced activities have age restrictions. Contact us for specific information about your age group.

Can I book group activities?

Absolutely! We specialize in group bookings for schools, corporate teams, and large parties. Contact our group booking team for customized packages and special rates.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your activity. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a fee. Weather-related cancellations will offer a full refund or reschedule.
